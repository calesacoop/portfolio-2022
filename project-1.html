Buster's Show of Love

Character development for a fictional animated T.V. show titled Buster’s Show of Love. Buster, the title character, a scrappy street dog, Beatrice, the main love interest, a sophisticated show dog, and Axel, Buster’s competition.

    About Me
    
      
    Hey, I'm Cale! An illustrator who draws characters with character, cause it takes one to know
        one. I’m inspired by the classics, both in animation and music. I’m consistent — last
        year, I listened to the same Elton John track 685 times, so I’ve got the dedication and
        patience to do just about anything. After 8 years as a dog groomer, I’ve learned the
        best way to connect with a client is to keep it short and neat, and to always  shake a
          paw on it.
